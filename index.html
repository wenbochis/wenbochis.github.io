NBA

Posted on 2023-05-07

The National Basketball Association, also known as the NBA, is a men’s professional basketball league composed of 30 professional teams in North America and one of the four major professional sports leagues in the United States.

Among them were born such as: Michael Jordan, LeBron James, Kobe Bryant, Karim Abdul Jabbar, Wilt Chamberlain, Bill Russell, Elvin Johnson, Larry Bird, Oscar Robertson, Shaquille O’Neal, John Havelichek, Rick Barry, John Stockton, Karl Malone, Elgin Baylor, David Robinson, Julius Erving Charles Barkley, Allen Iverson, James Harden, Yao Ming, Dennis Rodman, Tim Duncan, Dwyane Wade, Stephen Curry, Kevin Durant and other legendary players.

The NBA is divided into the Eastern Conference and the Western Conference, with each league divided into three divisions, each consisting of five teams. After the end of each season and before the start of the next season, there will be an NBA draft; The NBA summer league, NBA preseason, and NBA regular season games usually start in October. In February, there was a special performance event called the NBA All Star Game. After the NBA regular season ended, the Eastern and Western leagues advanced from the top eight to the playoffs, determining the Eastern and Western championships and advancing to the NBA Finals. The most outstanding player was awarded the Bill Russell Most Valuable Player Award in the NBA Finals.

James Harden, born in Los Angeles, California, USA on August 26, 1989, is an American professional basketball player, serving as a guard and playing for the Philadelphia 76ers in the NBA.

James Harden entered the NBA through draft in 2009 and successively served in the Thunder Team, the Rockets, the Nets and the 76ers. He was selected as the second best rookie lineup in the rookie season, the sixth best rookie in the 2011-12 season, the third best lineup in the 2012-13 season, the MVP of the regular season in the 2017-18 season, the NBA scoring champion for three times, the NBA assists champion for two times, the first best lineup for six times, and the all-star lineup for ten times, Selected as one of the top 75 NBA superstars in October 2021.
